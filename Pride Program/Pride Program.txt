ALLY/a term for someone who supports LGBTQ+ people. Allies might engage in events standing up for the LGBTQ+ community, like marches or protest for LGBTQ+ rights. An ally could be anyone of any sexuality or gender, even heterosexual. Heterosexual allies are commonly called straight allies.
STRAIGHT/a term for someone that is attracted to the opposite gender romantically and physically. For example, a person who identifies as male could call themselves straight if they're attracted to people that identify as female. Straight is a simple term for HETEROSEXUAL.
